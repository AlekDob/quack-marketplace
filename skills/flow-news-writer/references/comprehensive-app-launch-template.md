- First-time iOS app launches
- Apps with multiple features/modules
- Apps requiring enterprise certificate installation
- Strategic product launches

## Language Adaptation Notes

### Italian (IT)
- Use informal "tu" form
- Warm, enthusiastic tone
- "Siamo entusiasti", "La tua app"
- Technical terms: italianize where possible

### French (FR)
- Use formal "vous" form
- Professional, elegant tone
- "Nous sommes ravis", "Votre application"
- Avoid anglicisms

### English (EN)
- Active voice, direct
- Professional, friendly
- "We've launched", "Your app"
- American spelling
